# The Political Economy of AI Safety Regulation

Cowen argues that AI safety licensing regimes will be captured by incumbents, creating barriers to entry that protect large labs while slowing innovation. The metrics used to evaluate "safety" will become targets in the Goodhart sense — labs will optimize for passing safety evaluations rather than being actually safe.
