# Mechanism Design Theory

Survey of mechanism design theory (Hurwicz, Maskin, Myerson). Core concepts: incentive compatibility, revelation principle, implementation theory. The field designs "games" (mechanisms) where self-interested behavior leads to socially desirable outcomes.

Relevant to AI alignment because mechanism design assumes adversarial/strategic participants, while RLHF assumes cooperative ones. The adversarial assumption is more robust when dealing with optimization pressure.
