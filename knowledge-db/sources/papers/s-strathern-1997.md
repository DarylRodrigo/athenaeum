# Improving Ratings: Audit in the British University System

Strathern (1997). The paper that gave us the generalized form of Goodhart's Law: "When a measure becomes a target, it ceases to be a good measure." Applied to the British university audit system, showing how metrics designed to measure quality end up reshaping behavior in ways that satisfy the metric without improving the underlying quality.
